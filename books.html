Aq keme

Reading transports me to another universe where I converse with writers and their characters, experiencing entire lifetimes within just 2-4 weeks. I developed a passion for diverse books on my own initiative, beginning in 7th grade without any external pressure. My first book was "Rich Dad Poor Dad" by Robert Kiyosaki, which led me to read two more of his works, followed by 5-10 psychological books that helped me build strong mental resilience and emotional intelligence. Though I'm only 19 years old, people often remark that I possess the wisdom and perspective of someone in their thirties. This maturity stems from my deliberate choices in reading material, the mentors I've sought out, and the network of thoughtful individuals I've cultivated around me.
