Podcasts

Podcasts are one of the most valuable resources I regularly engage with. I typically listen to episodes that are at least 1.5 hours long, as I find this format allows for deep, meaningful conversations. What makes podcasts exceptional is the opportunity to hear directly from individuals who offer authentic life advice backed by real-world examples. Unlike more curated or edited content, podcasts provide unfiltered wisdom and experiences from people who have walked paths I aspire to follow, making them an invaluable tool for personal and professional growth.
